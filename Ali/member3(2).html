EDUCATION

I am first year student at ADA University. However, this is my second year at ADA, because I studied EAPP (English for Academic Purposes Program) for a year. My faculty is computer engineering and my graduation year is 2025.

WHY DID I CHOOSE ADA?

There are several reasons to choose ADA University. One of them is career opportunities which is mandatory for me. Second one is social life in this university. Social life is very wide and attractive. ADA provides students with different activities, seminars, webinars, and so on. The last reason is a little bit different than others. ADA one of the leader universities in Azerbaijan, and if you are ADA student you will have a chance to study abroad at least for a semester. Furthermore, almost every company all over the world accepts a diploma from ADA while hiring employees.
